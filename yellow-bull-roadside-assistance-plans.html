FAQ

What are the benefits of subscribing to Yellow Bull?

From S$120 to the mid-hundreds, the cost of a single tow or service call can add up quickly depending on the severity of the car fault.

For one flat fee, Yellow Bull's Roadside Assistance Plan covers you for these incidents up to 6 times a year with Family Cover and 4 times with Single Cover. This is a much more cost-effective option compared to purchasing them ala carte.

Also, our Family Cover is over 13% cheaper than what is currently available in the market. Don't let unexpected car troubles break the bank – join our plan today!

What happens if I need to cancel my plan?

Yellow Bull understands that circumstances can change. If you need to cancel your plan, you can do so at any time with no penalties or fees.

At Yellow Bull, we value relationships over commerce, and believe in providing everyone with the freedom and flexibility to manage their plans according to their preferences.

Are there any limitations to the coverage provided?

Yellow Bull offers comprehensive coverage within Singapore (Malaysia coming soon). However, it's important to note that certain exclusions and limitations may apply. We recommend reviewing the terms and conditions.

Can I use my plan for multiple vehicles?

No. Because we do even better — Yellow Bull Family Cover serves the vehicle (one license plate), and not the driver. This is important because your spouse or children are also included in our services, in cases where the car breaks down while they were driving it.
